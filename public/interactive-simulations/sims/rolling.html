The Physics

For a body rolling without slipping down an incline, acceleration depends on the moment of inertia ratio β = I/(mR²):

a = g sin θ / (1 + β)

Solid sphere β=2/5, hollow sphere 2/3, solid cylinder 1/2, hoop 1.
