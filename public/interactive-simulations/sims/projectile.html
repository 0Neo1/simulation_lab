The Physics

Projectile motion is a combination of constant horizontal velocity and constant downward acceleration due to gravity.

x(t) = v₀ cos(θ) · t
y(t) = v₀ sin(θ) · t − ½ g t²

The range is maximized at 45° (no air resistance). Try it!
