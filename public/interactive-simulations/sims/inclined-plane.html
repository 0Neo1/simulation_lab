The Physics

On an incline, gravity has a component mg sin θ down the slope and mg cos θ into the surface. Friction opposes motion: f = μN = μmg cos θ.

a = g(sin θ − μ cos θ)

The block stays at rest if tan θ ≤ μ_s.
