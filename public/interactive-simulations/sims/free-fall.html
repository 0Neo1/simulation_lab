The Physics

In a vacuum, all objects fall at the same rate. Air resistance is proportional to v² and depends on cross-sectional area, so light objects with large area (like feathers) reach terminal velocity quickly.

F_drag = ½ ρ C_d A v²
m·a = m·g − F_drag
