The Physics

Momentum is conserved in all collisions. Kinetic energy is conserved only in elastic collisions.

m₁v₁ + m₂v₂ = m₁v₁' + m₂v₂'
e = (v₂' − v₁') / (v₁ − v₂)

Coefficient of restitution: e=1 (elastic), e=0 (perfectly inelastic).
