Doppler Effect

Wavefronts compress in front of a moving source.

← All simulations

The Physics

When a source moves through a medium, wavefronts ahead of it compress (higher frequency) and those behind expand (lower frequency).

f' = f · (v_wave) / (v_wave − v_source · cos θ)

Mach 1 produces a shock wave; supersonic motion creates a Mach cone.
