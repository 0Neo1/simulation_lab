The Physics

Light bends when it crosses between media of different refractive index. The reflected ray makes an equal angle to the normal.

n₁ sin θ₁ = n₂ sin θ₂

If n₁ > n₂ and θ₁ > θ_c = arcsin(n₂/n₁), all light reflects (TIR).
