The Physics

Newton's law of gravitation creates an inverse-square attractive force. Orbits can be circular, elliptical, parabolic, or hyperbolic depending on energy.

F = G M m / r²

Circular orbit speed at radius r: v = √(GM/r). Escape speed: v_esc = √(2GM/r).
