The Physics

Three principal rays through a thin lens locate the image: parallel ray bends through F, central ray goes straight, focal-point ray emerges parallel.

1/d_o + 1/d_i = 1/f    M = −d_i/d_o

Positive f = converging lens. Image is real if d_i > 0, virtual if d_i < 0.
