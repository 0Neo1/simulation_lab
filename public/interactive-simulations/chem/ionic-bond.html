The Chemistry

Sodium (Na) has 1 valence electron; chlorine (Cl) needs 1 to complete its octet. Na transfers its electron to Cl, forming Na⁺ and Cl⁻ which attract electrostatically.

Na → Na⁺ + e⁻    Cl + e⁻ → Cl⁻    Na⁺ + Cl⁻ → NaCl
