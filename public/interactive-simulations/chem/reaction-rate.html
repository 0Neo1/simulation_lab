The Chemistry

Reactions occur only when particles collide with sufficient energy (activation energy E_a) and proper orientation. Higher temperature and concentration speed up reactions.

k = A · exp(−E_a / RT)

The Arrhenius equation: rate constant rises exponentially with temperature.
