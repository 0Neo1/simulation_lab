The Chemistry

Bohr proposed that electrons orbit the nucleus only at specific allowed energy levels. When an electron drops to a lower level, it emits a photon equal in energy to the difference.

E_n = −13.6/n² eV (hydrogen)
E_photon = E_high − E_low = h f

Visible spectral lines (Balmer series): transitions ending at n=2.
