Le Chatelier's Principle

Disturb an equilibrium and watch it shift back.

← All simulations

The Chemistry

For the reversible reaction A + B ⇌ C + D, equilibrium is reached when forward and reverse rates are equal. If you disturb the system (add reactant, remove product, change T), it shifts to counteract the change.

K_eq = [C][D] / [A][B]
