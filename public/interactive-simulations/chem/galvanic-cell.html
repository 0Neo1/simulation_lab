The Chemistry

The more reactive metal (Zn) gives up electrons (oxidation) at the anode; they flow through the external wire to the copper cathode where Cu²⁺ ions get reduced.

Anode: Zn → Zn²⁺ + 2e⁻
Cathode: Cu²⁺ + 2e⁻ → Cu
E°_cell = E°_cathode − E°_anode = 1.10 V

The salt bridge keeps both half-cells electrically neutral.
