The Chemistry

pH measures hydrogen ion concentration. Lower pH = more acidic, higher pH = more basic. Pure water is neutral (pH 7).

pH = −log₁₀[H⁺]
pH + pOH = 14

Each pH unit is a 10× change in [H⁺].
