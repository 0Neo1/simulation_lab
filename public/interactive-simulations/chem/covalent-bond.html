The Chemistry

In a covalent bond, atoms share electrons. Energy is minimum at the bond length: too close → strong repulsion; too far → no attraction.

U(r) = 4ε[(σ/r)¹² − (σ/r)⁶]

The Lennard-Jones potential captures this trade-off.
