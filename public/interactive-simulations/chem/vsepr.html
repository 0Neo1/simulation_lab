The Chemistry

VSEPR theory: electron pairs around a central atom arrange themselves to minimize repulsion. The number of bonded atoms and lone pairs determines the shape.

2 → linear (180°)
3 → trigonal planar (120°)
4 → tetrahedral (109.5°)
5 → trigonal bipyramidal
6 → octahedral (90°)
